Target Audience:

  • Any gender
  • 18 years of age and up
  • People who's jobs or livelihood are
    effected by the current weather

    • Two Personas:

    Persona One

  • a young man named Luis
  • 19 years of age
  • an exterior painter working for Pro-Painters
  • saving up to pay for tution in the
    coming semester at BYU
  • is tasked with checking the weather
    during the week coming up to the assignment
    to see if the weather will be working
    conditions during their time at the location

    • Persona Two

  • a woman named Sarah
  • 64 years of age
  • loves working on her garden
  • was a stay at home mother for about 28 years
  • has been alone with her husband for 10 years
    now after her children moved out
  • checks the weather to see if it is working condition

    • Scenarios:

  • What is the weather like right now?
  • Do I need to wear a coat or a sweater while
    I take my dog out?
  • What is the weather going to be this Thursday
    as I drive across the country?
  • Is it raining or snowing outside?
  • Will I have to shovel the sidewalk
    tomorrow before work?
